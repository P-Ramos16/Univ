Welcome to the DesignWorks Professional Tutorial!

The online manual is divided into a number of tutorial sections to help you get started quickly with the package and get the most out of the powerful features available. Here are the sections available now:

  • The 5-Minute Schematic Diagram - This covers creating a new design, placing and wiring device symbols, advanced signal connections by name and by power and ground symbol, using various editing features.
  • Advanced Schematic Editing - This tutorial covers navigating around a schematic, creating busses, adding text notations, and automatic gate packaging.
  • Creating Device Symbols and Libraries - This page shows you how to create and edit your own device symbols and store them in library files.
