II. - OTHER KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS
------------------------------

Delete Key - Deletes selected items
Arrow Keys - Flip and rotate items while placing
             a device or pasting
Arrow Keys - Move selected items in small steps
Ctrl, Shift, Alt - Control line breaks while drawing signals
Ctrl, Shift - Control device alignment while placing devices
Page Up Key - Scroll Up
Page Up + Ctrl - Scroll Left
Page Down Key - Scroll Down
Page Down + Ctrl - Scroll Right
